How to Play

Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character (red circle).

Collect green targets while avoiding red obstacles and the walls.

Look for power-ups to help your journey:

  • Blue (S): Speed boost
  • Purple (P): Shield protection
  • Orange (T): Extra time
  • Gold ($): Bonus points
